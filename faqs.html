Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the minimum credits?
16 credits
What are the maximum credits?
30 credits (not including NPTEL or external courses)
Can we take classes during both A1 and A2 slots?
Yes, but you will have a hectic timetable and 2 exams on the same day during CATS.
Can we change our selected faculty after clicking the register button?
Yes, you can change it within your allotted course registration time, open slot time, or also during add and drop time.
